Cohort Expectations
🛠️ Refine your ideathon projects—whether ideas, prototypes, or MVPs—into complete, well-built solutions with strong demos. Clearly address a real problem, explain your value to the Canton ecosystem, and show why your product is essential and worth deeper exploration by Canton and its partners.

Make sure your project includes the fundamentals:
A working demo
A well-supported and clearly explained solution
A defined problem statement and rationale
A strong value proposition and real-world relevance
To successfully graduate from this mentorship, at least 1 representative from the team would need to attend 80% of all the sessions planned.
